About the Game


Space Invaders Game is all about using the perfect timing and perfect aim to shoot all the inavders before they get to you.

Finish all the invaders using rockets/lasers before they reach your shooter and destroy it.

It is a single player game where you can see the score in real time as you shoot down the mighty aliens who are trying to catch you.

Difficulty Modes

Easy

This is the most basic mode, you have to shoot down all 20 invaders.

Medium

In this mode, the number of invaders increases and so do their speed as they try to reach you.

Hard

This is the hardest mode. Here the number of invaders and their speed increases. Furthermore, the invaders get blipped in between as they reach the shooter.


Can you finish them all before they reach you?
